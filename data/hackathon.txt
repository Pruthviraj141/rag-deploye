INTRODUCTION
------------
PICT InC (Impetus and Concepts) is the flagship technical event of Pune Institute of Computer Technology. It is an international-level inter-collegiate competition designed to promote innovation, creativity, and technical excellence among engineering students.

Over the years, InC has evolved from a small inter-departmental competition into a nationally and internationally recognized platform that attracts students, academicians, startups, and industry professionals.

InC bridges the gap between academia and industry by providing students with opportunities to present real-world projects, receive expert feedback, gain mentorship, and secure internships or job offers through direct interaction with industry leaders.

OBJECTIVES OF InC
-----------------
The objectives of InC are to:
- Provide a platform for emerging technical talent
- Encourage innovative thinking and problem-solving
- Facilitate interaction between students and industry professionals
- Promote entrepreneurship among students
- Transform academic projects into real-world deployable solutions

EVENTS AT InC 2026
------------------
InC 2026 consists of four major events:
1. CONCEPTS – Final Year Project Exhibition
2. IMPETUS – Project Competition for FE, SE, and TE
3. PRADNYA – Programming Contest
4. TECHFIESTA – International Hackathon

========================================================
CONCEPTS — FINAL YEAR PROJECT COMPETITION
========================================================

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
- Only Final Year (BE) Engineering students
- All engineering branches allowed
- All team members must belong to the same institute

TEAM STRUCTURE
--------------
- Minimum 1 member
- Maximum 5 members

REGISTRATION FEE
----------------
INR 500 per team

PROJECT REQUIREMENTS
--------------------
- Project must be fully implemented and functional
- Idea-only or partially completed projects are not allowed
- Project must demonstrate innovation, feasibility, and real-world impact

DOMAINS
-------
- Application Development
- Web Applications
- Mobile Applications (Android)
- Database Systems
- Computer Networks
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Information and Network Security
- Digital Signal Processing
- Image, Speech, and Video Processing
- Embedded Systems
- VLSI Systems
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Pattern Recognition
- Bio Signal Processing
- Biomedical Applications
- Bioinformatics
- Emerging and interdisciplinary technologies

EVALUATION PROCESS
------------------
- Minimum 3 industry judges per project
- Evaluation may occur in single or multiple sessions
- Teams must attend all assigned sessions

JUDGING CRITERIA
----------------
- Innovation and originality
- Technical complexity and feasibility
- Quality of implementation
- Scalability and real-world relevance
- Presentation and demonstration quality

PRESENTATION MODE
-----------------
- Pune participants: Offline at PICT campus
- Outside Pune: Online via Microsoft Teams or Google Meet

RULES
-----
- All team members must be present
- Absence in any assigned session leads to disqualification
- If domain entries are insufficient, only e-certificates will be issued
- Judges’ and organizers’ decisions are final

PRADNYA — PROGRAMMING CONTEST
========================================================

ELIGIBILITY
-----------
Junior Category:
- First Year and Second Year undergraduate students

Senior Category:
- Third Year and Final Year undergraduate students

TEAM STRUCTURE
--------------
- Maximum 2 members per team

REGISTRATION FEE
----------------
INR 100 per team

EVENT STRUCTURE
---------------
Round 1: MCQ Round
- Programming fundamentals, logic, problem-solving
- Pune district: Offline
- Outside Pune: Hybrid

Wildcard Round
- Open to junior and senior categories
- Top 5 teams from each category qualify directly

Round 2: Programming Round
- Timed coding contest
- Any programming language allowed
- Different problem sets for junior and senior

Round 3: Judging Round
- Top 5 teams per category evaluated
- Judging based on correctness, efficiency, and approach
- Top 3 teams selected per category

RULES
-----
- Pune participants: Offline mandatory
- Outside Pune: Hybrid allowed
- Judges’ decisions are final
