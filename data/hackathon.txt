PICT InC 2026 event dates are March 20, 2026, March 21, 2026, and March 22, 2026.

PICT InC 2026 is organized by Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), Pune.

PRADNYA is the programming contest at PICT InC 2026.

PRADNYA has two categories: Junior Category and Senior Category.

PRADNYA Junior Category is for First Year and Second Year undergraduate students.

PRADNYA Senior Category is for Third Year and Final Year undergraduate students.

PRADNYA maximum team size is 2 members per team.

PRADNYA registration fee is INR 100 per team.

PRADNYA Round 1 is the MCQ round testing programming fundamentals, logic, and problem-solving skills.

PRADNYA Round 1 presentation mode for Pune participants is offline.

PRADNYA Round 1 presentation mode for outside Pune participants is hybrid mode.

PRADNYA Wildcard Round is open to both Junior and Senior categories.

PRADNYA Wildcard Round: Top five teams from each category may qualify directly.

PRADNYA Round 2 is the Programming Round, a timed coding contest.

PRADNYA Round 2 allows any programming language.

PRADNYA Round 2 has different problem sets for Junior and Senior categories.

PRADNYA Round 3 is the Judging Round for the top five teams from each category.

PRADNYA Round 3 evaluation is based on correctness, efficiency, and approach.

PRADNYA Round 3: Top three teams are selected per category.

PRADNYA rule: Pune participants must attend offline.

PRADNYA rule: Outside Pune participants are allowed hybrid mode.

PRADNYA rule: Judges' decisions are final.
